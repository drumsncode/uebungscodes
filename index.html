Bio!

THE ACADEMY, established in 2020 by Justus Horn and Kamil Mnich, are two musicians who came together to elevate Modern Rock with their powerful and distinctive sound to a new level. In their musical repertoire, THE ACADEMY combines juicy riffs with catchy melodies that indelibly burn themselves into memory. Their music is more than just an auditory experience; it is a journey through a broad spectrum of emotions and moods, touching the soul and stimulating the mind. Justus’s precise and energetic drumming forms the rhythmic foundation upon which Kamil unfolds his layered guitar sounds and charismatic vocals. Together, they create a musical landscape that is both raw and sophisticated, a rare combination that is seldom found in today’s music scene.
